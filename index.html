My artistic journey began through dance. I was a professional dancer from 2009 to 2012. I have danced in music videos with Willow Smith and performed backup for shows such as America's Got Talent. My classical training began back in 2003, training in ballet, jazz, tap, contemporary, and ballroom.

Through dance I was able to discover the world of acting in 2012. I have appeared in national commercials such as Audi and ExxonMobil, and I continued my acting pursuits through auditioning and acting classes up until 2015 where I decided to focus on my educational pursuits.

Entering college I rediscovered my love for the arts and decided to pursue a degree in Film, which is where I currently study at the University of California, Riverside.
